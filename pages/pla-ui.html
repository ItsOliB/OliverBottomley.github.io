Project LA - UI Design

UI/UX Design - Designs of UI apps and websites.

This project was focused around making UI elements such as a speedometer, status HUD and location display. I made this for a project which is linked into the Project LA branding. I designed it to be simplistic and not cluttered. I took reference from other designs such as Nopixelâ€™s HUD design with the radial status hud, the look of it overall just makes the overall user-experience better and easier to see all information that the player will need.

The character selector and creator followed a grey and white colour scheme, I wanted to make sure it was consistent with both the hud and inventory. Even though the character and spawn selector is blue (which follows the Project LA branding) I wanted to throw out different colour schemes from both the branding and the HUD.
