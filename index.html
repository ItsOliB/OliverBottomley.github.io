About me

I’m 22 years old and currently studying UI/UX Design at Norwich University of the Arts. I started out in photography, which then led me to creating logos and branding for my own projects. Over time, this grew into freelancing within the Grand Theft Auto V modification (FiveM) community, where I design branding and user interfaces. Now, I’m looking to build on this experience and take on more freelance work in UI/UX design. I’m passionate about creating clear, engaging designs that help brands and users connect.

My Speciality
