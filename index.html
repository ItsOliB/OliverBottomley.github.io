About me

I've been interested in the creative industry for years. It started out with Photography, making logos and designs for myself and friends and then it evolved into me working in the Grand Theft Auto V Modification (FiveM) Community. The feeling of creativity when designing is so fulfilling, trying out different styles, colours or patterns until you produce your final piece. What inspires me is the growth of the industry, seeing so many new designers display their art in different ways and art styles. It's so easy to be at an impasse with your current project, and seeing others' work could spark ideas and creativity.
